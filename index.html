Mozilla is cool

Mozilla Firefox, or simply Firefox, is a free and open-source web browser developed by the Mozilla Foundation and its subsidiary, the Mozilla Corporation. It uses the Gecko rendering engine to display web pages, which implements current and anticipated web standards.

At Mozilla, we're a global community of :

  • technologists
  • thinkers
  • builders

working together to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide canbe informed, contributors, and creators of the Web. We believe this act of human collaboration across an open platform is essential to individual growth and our collective future.
